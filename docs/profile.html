About

I work at the intersection of statistics, AI, and organizational strategy. With a background in statistical consulting and collaboration, I use data to help teams define and navigate their toughest problems. My natural curiosity allows me to learn new domains quickly and implement intentional solutions that are grounded in the client’s mission.

Maximizing impact and engaging deeply with clients is the central topic of my research. Learn more about the project here.
